About Me

Ethan began his audio career over 5 years ago while a student at Arts Institute. With a focus on audio production and recording throughout school Ethan was able to build the solid foundation his audio work rests upon today. Soon after college, Ethan began working both in live audio production and audio post production. He has worked on a variety of projects ranging from album recording to professional video productions. In 2017, Ethan worked as a full-time Post-production Audio Engineer at Elevation Church in Charlotte, NC. Since then he has been a part of numerous video productions at Elevation Church and continues to do so in his freelance work.
