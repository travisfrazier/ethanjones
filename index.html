About Me

Ethan began his audio career over 5 years ago while a student at The Arts Institute in Atlanta, GA. With a focus on audio production and recording throughout school Ethan was able to build the solid foundation his audio work rests upon today. Soon after college, Ethan began working both in live audio production and studio recording. He has worked on a variety of projects ranging from album recording to professional video productions. In the beginning of 2016, Ethan began working full-time as a Post-production Audio Engineer at Elevation Church in Charlotte, NC. Since then he has been a part of numerous video productions at Elevation Church and in his freelance work.
